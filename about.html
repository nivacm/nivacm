Who am I?

I am a migrant woman, who loves codes, maths and art .

About Me

Throughout my life, I have felt at home in various places, situations, and eras. My involvement with mathematics allows me to constantly gain a deeper understanding of abstract theories in which the absolute truth can be discovered. As I have immersed myself in a variety of languages over the last few years, I am feeling the learning process will be always part of my life. I can see myself as someone who is active and committed, eager to reach new heights. The art and reflections with other migrants have allowed me to appreciate myself as a migrant woman and believe that inclusion benefits all. My migration led me to She Codes, a place that prioritizes inclusion more than integration. I am feel proud to be part of the She Codes Plus cohort. She Codes now has become my new bridge to discovering myself in a new role, a new adventure, as a junior software developer.
